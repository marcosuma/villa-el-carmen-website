An Authentic Apulian Experience

Nestled amidst the serene landscapes of Puglia, Villa El Carmen offers a unique blend of rustic charm and modern luxury. With 3 bedrooms and 3 bathrooms, our villa is perfect for families and groups. It's a destination where you can unwind, reconnect with nature, and create memories that will last a lifetime.

Historic Charm

The heart of our villa is a beautifully restored ancient lamia, preserving the authentic soul of Puglia.

The Ancient Lamia

Experience the unique character of staying in a traditional Apulian stone structure, updated with every modern comfort. The thick walls and classic architecture provide a cool and tranquil retreat from the summer sun.

The Azure Pool

Immerse yourself in our stunning 40m² swimming pool. Flanked by comfortable loungers and kissed by the Italian sun, it’s the perfect spot to cool off, relax with a book, or simply float your cares away while gazing at the olive groves.

Our Ancient Olive Grove

The villa is enveloped by acres of ancient olive trees. Take a stroll through the groves and breathe in the fragrant air. From these very trees, we produce our own exquisite, cold-pressed olive oil – a true taste of Puglia.

Liquid Gold: Our Signature Olive Oil

At Villa El Carmen, we are proud to share our passion for authentic Apulian flavors. Our extra virgin olive oil is lovingly crafted from olives hand-picked from our own groves. It's a gift from our land to your table.

Guests are welcome to a complimentary bottle to enjoy during their stay and purchase more to take home.

Apulian Feasts & Alfresco Dining

Happy Hour with Friends

Gather your favorite people as the sun begins to set. The terrace is the perfect stage for clinking glasses of Aperol Spritz, sharing local taralli, and creating joyful memories together.

Wine O'Clock by the Pool

There's no better way to unwind than with a crisp glass of local white wine by the shimmering pool. Let the Apulian sun warm your skin as you sip, relax, and float your cares away.

Dine with Your Private Chef

For a truly unforgettable evening, live the experience of private chefs cooking local food. Indulge in an exclusive multi-course meal crafted from the finest local ingredients, all in the comfort of your own villa.
